About us

Far more than a swim school

We started teaching Cornwall to swim back in 2002, and we've grown a lot since. Alongside lessons for every age, Fitness 4 U is now home to aquatic fitness, rehabilitation and wellbeing classes, chair-based sessions, a dedicated sensory pool and sports massage — all under one roof.

Our warm, accessible pool in St Columb Major is within easy reach of Newquay, Truro, Bodmin, Wadebridge and beyond. Whoever you are and wherever you're starting from — nervous beginner, returning swimmer, recovering from surgery, or just here for the fun of it — there's a place for you in the water.

Friendly, inclusive and led by experienced instructors. Everyone's welcome, every ability catered for.

Good to know

Questions parents ask

A few common ones — there's always more we're happy to talk through.

Does my child need armbands?

No. If they'd like to wear them at first that's absolutely fine — we'll work with them and take the armbands off as soon as they're ready.

Should my child eat before swimming?

Please don't eat within an hour of swimming. Eating any later than that can bring on cramp or sickness in the water.

Can we take photos or videos?

Not during lessons, for child safeguarding. We can arrange a dedicated photo session though — including underwater shots and video. Just ask at reception.

Should I take my child swimming between lessons?

Yes — the more time they spend in the water, the quicker their confidence builds and the faster they improve.

How long will it take my child to learn?

Every child is different. Little ones who've come since babyhood often swim unaided around two. Most can manage 10m on their front and back by about five with regular lessons.

My child is nervous about coming. What should I do?

Let us know — it's really common, even well into lessons. Perseverance works best, and it helps us to know what they're finding tricky. Everything's treated in the strictest confidence.
